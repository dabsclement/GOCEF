GOCEF Leadership Training Workshop

This is a 7 months program. The Certificate Participants engaged in research, article write ups, and discussing topics such as Differences between Bribery/Embezzlement/Extortion/Tips and their effect on a Society, Aspiring to make a Difference, Different Leadership Methods, Effective Team Management etc. The Certificate Participants are also required to read at least 3 books during the 7 months, and write journal on the book. The books are usually in the categories such as: Discovering Purpose, Leadership Methods, Team management, Self Development etc. Speakers at the workshop are usually experts from different fields holding interactive sessions on some important issues

GOCEF Mentoring Scheme

Volunteers from GOCEF Leadership Club go to secondary schools to mentor younger generations in becoming a patriotic citizen as well as effective leaders. In order for this to be effective, we engaged both the Teachers and the Students in the workshops. The training for the teachers are on different days from the students.

Skill Acquisition Program

Fashion Training Program This was started in 2015 in collaboration with Glorious Trinity Fashion to teach tailoring for 6 months and the best student was sponsored by GOCEF to go for additional 9 months training and GOCEF presented her with a brand new sewing machine at the end of the full training. As time goes on, GOCEF was able to get volunteer trainers to train people at GOCEF Center. At each Training session, there’s 1 hour interactive discussions on Business/Life Skills or Some challenges facing Nigeria that affect growth of businesses.

Ankara Craft: Participants learnt how to use Ankara to make bags, shoes, bangles, hand fans, head wrap etc. Everything on display was made by the participants of GOCEF Vocational Training participants with no prior experience.

Bead Making: Everything on display was made by the participants of GOCEF Vocational Training participants with no prior experience

CH3 - Caring Heart, Helping Hand

This is GOCEF’s responses to unplanned situations that need immediate attention. Such as homeless person getting assistance, someone in dire needs of medical treatment or purchase of medications, being notified of individual or family sleeping in hunger with no hope of having a meal, not able to feed during lockdown due to COVID-19 etc.

COVID-19 Palliatives: Cash (N1,500) each was transferred to 76 individuals during total lockdown that was due to COVID-9. Also, food, face mask, and hand sanitizer were given to 50 recipients during the partial lockdown.

About Us

Grace Oluwakemi Christian Empowerment Foundation (GOCEF), formerly known as Grace Oluwakemi Christian Institute is an NGO in Lagos, Nigeria, and was established on June 22, 2013. GOCEF was built on the legacy of Late Evangelist Grace Oluwakemi Olayoriju nee Oluata. GOCEF majorly focuses on alleviation of poverty, jungle justice, and corruption. We do this through skill acquisition programs, assistance in education, Petty trade funding, reorient people through seminars/workshops, and mentorship. Our programs/services are 100% free of charge for the past 7+ years. It’s easily accessible to less privileged.
