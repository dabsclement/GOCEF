About GOCEF

Grace Oluwakemi Christian Empowerment Foundation (GOCEF), formerly known as Grace Oluwakemi Christian Institute is an NGO in Lagos, Nigeria, and was established on June 22, 2013. GOCEF was built on the legacy of Late Evangelist Grace Oluwakemi Olayoriju nee Oluata. GOCEF majorly focuses on alleviation of poverty, jungle justice, and corruption. We do this through skill acquisition programs, assistance in education, Petty trade funding, reorient people through seminars/workshops, and mentorship.
